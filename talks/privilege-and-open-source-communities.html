Exploring Privilege in Open Source Communities (OSCON London 2016)

Resources:

While I was working on this talk, I read a lot of blog posts, issue threads, pull request threads, listened to a few podcasts, watched a bunch of videos, and much more. I wanted to give credit to the creators, and provide resources for others to think more about these topics. If you are one of the creators listed, feel free to submit a pull request this page to link back to yourself. Also, to anyone who I have talked to about this topic, thank you for your time. <3

For my first major conference talk that was longer than 25 mintues, I am pretty excited how it turned out.
